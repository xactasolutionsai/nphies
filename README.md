# NAFES

**Healthcare Management System for Saudi Arabia**

---

## About

NAFES is a modern healthcare management platform designed to streamline medical insurance operations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The system connects healthcare providers with insurance companies through NPHIES (National Platform for Health Information Exchange Services), enabling real-time verification and seamless claim processing.

## What We Do

### Insurance Eligibility
Instantly verify patient insurance coverage and benefits before providing medical services. Know exactly what's covered, copay amounts, and remaining limits.

### Prior Authorization
Submit and track medical authorization requests digitally. Reduce paperwork and get faster approvals for procedures and treatments.

### Claims Processing
Submit healthcare claims electronically and track their status in real-time. Faster processing means faster payments.

### Benefits Management
View comprehensive coverage details including:
- Annual limits and remaining balances
- Copay percentages and maximum amounts
- Coverage for consultations, surgeries, dental, vision, and more
- Network restrictions and special conditions

## Why NAFES?

- **Speed** - Real-time insurance verification in seconds
- **Accuracy** - Direct integration with NPHIES reduces errors
- **Compliance** - Fully compliant with Saudi healthcare regulations
- **Efficiency** - Reduce administrative workload and paperwork
- **Transparency** - Clear visibility into coverage and claims status

## NPHIES Integration

NAFES is fully integrated with Saudi Arabia's national health information exchange platform, ensuring:
- Standardized data exchange using FHIR R4
- Secure communication with insurance providers
- Up-to-date benefit information
- Compliant claim submissions
